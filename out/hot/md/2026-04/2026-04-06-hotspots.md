### 3. Google Launches AI Edge Gallery for On-Device Gemma 4 and Gemma 3 Models  [Analysis]

- Category: Product Release
- Sources: Simon Willison's Blog

Google is productizing local inference on consumer devices, which matters for privacy-sensitive deployments, lower serving costs, and broader adoption of Gemma as an edge AI stack.

**Key takeaways:**
- AI Edge Gallery is Google’s official app for running Gemma models directly on phones, according to the cited App Store listing and analysis.
- The app supports Gemma 4 E2B and E4B variants, plus selected Gemma 3-family models, making it a concrete distribution channel for Google’s open model line.
- The release shifts Gemma from a model family developers download manually to a packaged end-user experience, which could accelerate experimentation with offline and low-latency AI use cases.

**Sources:**
- [Google AI Edge Gallery](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Apr/6/google-ai-edge-gallery) (Simon Willison's Blog)
