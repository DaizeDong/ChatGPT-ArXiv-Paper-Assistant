### 4. Baidu Releases ERNIE-Image, an 8B Open-Weight Text-to-Image Model  [GitHub]

- Category: Product Release
- Sources: GitHub Trending Repos

An 8B open-weight image model with a state-of-the-art claim could shift enterprise and startup build-vs-buy decisions in generative media.

**Key takeaways:**
- Baidu published ERNIE-Image as an open text-to-image model rather than a closed API-only system, increasing its relevance for self-hosted and fine-tuned deployments.

- The repository description claims state-of-the-art performance among open-weight text-to-image models, positioning ERNIE-Image directly against other open image generators rather than proprietary systems.
