- Category: Product Release
- Sources: ByteDance Seed

ByteDance is expanding its model stack beyond text into real-time voice interaction, a strategic move for consumer assistants, multimodal products, and China-based model competition.

**Key takeaways:**
- ByteDance Seed officially announced Seed 2.0 as a new model release through its own channels, making this a confirmed product launch rather than a rumor or benchmark leak.
- The company separately released Dola-Seed-2.0-Preview on Arena, signaling an intent to expose the model to public comparative evaluation and developer attention.
- ByteDance also introduced a Seed Full-Duplex Speech LLM designed for simultaneous conversational turn-taking, with stated emphasis on attentive listening and robust interference suppression.
- The combination of Seed 2.0 plus a full-duplex speech model suggests ByteDance is targeting native voice agents and multimodal assistant experiences rather than only chatbot-style text inference.
