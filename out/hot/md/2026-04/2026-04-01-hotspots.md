### 1. Time-series foundation models are getting more serious with a decoder-only, long-context approach

- Category: Research
- Scores: final=5.923 quality=6 heat=5 importance=6
- Sources: Hacker News, Hugging Face Trending Papers

Time-series forecasting is a large commercial category, but foundation-model progress here has lagged language and vision. A 200M-parameter decoder-only model with 16k context suggests the field is testing whether LLM-style scaling and long-context modeling can generalize to forecasting workloads.

Key takeaways:
- The work drew attention from both Hacker News and Hugging Face Trending Papers, indicating interest beyond a niche paper release.
- The model’s decoder-only design and long context window point to a push toward more general-purpose sequence modeling for forecasting.
- If results hold up, this could matter for demand forecasting, operations, finance, and industrial monitoring where long-range temporal structure is important.

Representative sources:
