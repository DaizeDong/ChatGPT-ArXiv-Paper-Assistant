### 3. NVIDIA introduces Dynamo for agentic coding inference workloads  [Official]

- Category: Product Release
- Sources: NVIDIA AI

If agentic coding becomes a major inference category, infrastructure optimized for KV-cache reuse and high-call-volume sessions could materially change serving economics and platform selection.

**Key takeaways:**
- NVIDIA framed agentic coding as a distinct workload where a single session can trigger hundreds of API calls, making traditional inference stacks inefficient.
- Dynamo is positioned around KV-aware routing, directly targeting wasted recomputation and context-handling bottlenecks that increase cost per token.
- The announcement matters less as a model launch than as a stack-level optimization play for coding agents, where infrastructure advantage can become a product moat.

**Sources:**
- [Traditional inference wasn’t built for agentic coding](https://x.com/nvidiaai/status/2048069526000934986) (NVIDIA AI [Primary])
